Instructions

Your bonus payment will depend on how many of the 16 objects you are able to correctly categorize: the greater the number of objects that you place correctly, the greater your bonus payment (+2 cents per item); and the more you get wrong, the lower your bonus payment (-2 cents per item).



For instance, if you correctly categorize 11 of the 16 objects and categorize 5 incorrectly, then your bonus would be 11 * 2 - 5 * 2 = 12 cents.
