Instructions

In this experiment, you will be repeatedly shown objects, initially placed in the center of the screen. Each object is secretly a member of one of two categories (A and B), represented by a bin above (category A) and a bin below (category B) the objects. Your job is to figure out which category each object belongs to. You will get a bonus depending on your ability to correctly categorize each object.



You can make up to a $0.32 bonus on each trial: each correct guess will earn you $0.02; however, each incorrect guess will lose you $0.02 (if you have a negative bonus at the end of the experiment, your bonus will be set to $0.00).
