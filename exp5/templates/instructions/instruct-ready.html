Instructions

Your bonus payment will depend on how many of the objects you are able to correctly categorize: the greater the number of objects that you place correctly, the greater your bonus payment (+2 cents per item); and the more you get wrong, the lower your bonus payment (-2 cents per item).



For instance, if you correctly categorize 8 of the 11 objects and categorize 3 incorrectly, then your bonus would be 8 * 2 - 3 * 2 = 10 cents.
