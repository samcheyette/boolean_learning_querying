Instructions

In each trial there will be two categories, A and B, represented by a bin at the top of the screen (category A) and a bin at the bottom of the screen (category B). We will give you a couple random examples of objects in category A and category B and your job is to figure out which category new objects belong to. You will get a bonus depending on your ability to correctly categorize each new object.



Each correct guess will earn you $0.02; however, each incorrect guess will lose you $0.02 (if you have a negative bonus at the end of the experiment, your bonus will be set to $0.00).
